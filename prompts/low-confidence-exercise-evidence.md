# OpenEvidence Prompt — Evidence for Common Rehabilitation Exercises

I'm looking for the current evidence on dosing, EMG activation, and clinical effectiveness for the following rehabilitation exercises. For each exercise, please provide:

1. **EMG activation data** — which muscles and at what % MVIC
2. **Evidence-based dosing** — sets, reps, frequency, intensity from RCTs or systematic reviews
3. **Clinical effectiveness** — what patient outcomes does the evidence support?
4. **Key coaching cues** supported by biomechanical research
5. **Evidence-based regressions and progressions**

Please cite specific studies (author, year, journal) for each.

---

## Section 1: Exercises With Limited Evidence in My Knowledge

### 1. Wrist Radial/Ulnar Deviation Exercise
Seated with forearm on thigh, wrist hanging off edge, moving wrist side to side with light weight.
- What does research say about effective dosing for wrist deviator strengthening?
- Is this exercise studied in clinical trials for lateral epicondylalgia, de Quervain's, or wrist instability?
- What loads and rep ranges show meaningful strength gains?
- Are there EMG studies comparing radial vs ulnar deviation muscle activation patterns?

### 2. Seated Hip Internal Rotation Exercise
Sitting on edge of chair, rotating the shin outward to internally rotate the hip.
- Is this a clinically recommended exercise for hip IR strengthening, or are there better alternatives?
- What muscles does this actually target based on EMG?
- Which patient populations benefit from isolated hip IR exercise (post-THA, FAI, hip OA)?
- Recommended dosing from clinical studies?

## Section 2: Exercises Needing Stronger Evidence

### 3. Wrist Flexor Stretch
Standard wrist flexor stretch with arm extended, fingers pulled back.
- Optimal hold duration and frequency from stretch research?
- Evidence for effectiveness in lateral epicondylalgia or carpal tunnel syndrome?
- Does stretching produce actual tissue lengthening or primarily neurological tolerance change?

### 4. Wrist Extensor Stretch
Standard wrist extensor stretch.
- Specific evidence for use in lateral epicondylalgia (tennis elbow)?
- What do Martinez-Silvestrini et al. and Bisset et al. report on stretching vs exercise for lateral elbow tendinopathy?

### 5. Forearm Pronation/Supination with Hammer
Gripping a hammer at end of handle and rotating forearm through full range.
- EMG data for pronator teres, pronator quadratus, supinator, and biceps during this exercise?
- Is this used in evidence-based protocols for forearm or elbow rehabilitation?
- Dosing recommendations from clinical trials?

### 6. Thumb Opposition Exercise
Touching thumb to each fingertip in sequence.
- EMG activation of thenar muscles during opposition tasks?
- Evidence for use in CMC OA, median nerve recovery, or post-surgical hand rehabilitation?
- Dosing and progression evidence from the hand therapy literature?

### 7. Sidelying Hip Adduction
Sidelying with top leg supported, lifting the bottom leg.
- What % MVIC does this achieve for adductor longus, magnus, and gracilis?
- Is this exercise recommended in groin pain or adductor strain rehabilitation protocols?
- How does it compare to the Copenhagen adductor exercise in muscle activation levels?

### 8. Cervicothoracic Self-Mobilization
Self-mobilization techniques targeting the cervicothoracic junction.
- What specific techniques have RCT support?
- Dosing: frequency, duration, repetitions from the evidence?
- Is self-mobilization as effective as manual therapy alone for this region?

### 9. Schroth-Based Three-Dimensional Exercises
Schroth method exercises for thoracic postural correction.
- What RCTs support Schroth exercises beyond Özdemir 2023?
- Specific dosing protocols from the literature?
- Effect sizes for kyphosis angle reduction, pain, and quality of life?
- How does Schroth compare to general exercise for thoracic hyperkyphosis?

### 10. Hip Flexor and Hamstring Stretching
Combined hip flexor (Thomas position) and hamstring stretching program.
- Optimal stretch duration and frequency from systematic reviews?
- Evidence that hip flexor stretching improves lumbar lordosis or anterior pelvic tilt?
- Does hamstring flexibility correlate with low back pain outcomes?
- What do Moreside & McGill recommend from their biomechanics research?

### 11. Posterior Chain Strengthening (Hamstring and Calf Exercises)
Hamstring curls and calf raise variations.
- For hamstring exercises: EMG comparison of prone curl vs seated curl vs Nordic hamstring
- For calf raises: single-leg vs bilateral activation differences
- Evidence-based progression from bodyweight to loaded
- Dosing differences for tendinopathy vs general strengthening vs post-ACL rehabilitation
