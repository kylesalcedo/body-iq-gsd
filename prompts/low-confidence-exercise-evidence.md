# OpenEvidence Prompt — Low-Confidence Exercise Evidence

The following rehabilitation exercises in our database lack sufficient evidence for dosing, EMG activation data, and clinical effectiveness. For each exercise, please provide:

1. **EMG activation data** — target muscles and % MVIC where studies exist
2. **Evidence-based dosing** — sets, reps, frequency, intensity from RCTs or systematic reviews
3. **Clinical effectiveness** — what outcomes does the evidence support?
4. **Key coaching cues** supported by biomechanical research
5. **Regressions and progressions** with evidence rationale

Please cite specific studies (author, year, journal) for each.

---

## Tier 1: Lowest Confidence (0.75) — Need Most Evidence

### 1. Wrist Deviation Exercise (Radial/Ulnar)
Seated with forearm on thigh, wrist hanging off edge. Move wrist side to side with light weight.
- What does research say about effective dosing for wrist deviator strengthening?
- Is this exercise used in clinical trials for lateral epicondylalgia, de Quervain's, or wrist instability?
- What loads and rep ranges show meaningful strength gains?
- Are there EMG studies comparing radial vs ulnar deviation muscle activation patterns?

### 2. Seated Hip Internal Rotation
Sit on edge of chair with feet on floor. Rotate the shin outward to internally rotate the hip.
- Is this a clinically recommended exercise, or is there a better alternative for hip IR strengthening?
- What muscles does this actually target based on EMG?
- What populations benefit from isolated hip IR exercise (post-THA, FAI, hip OA)?
- Recommended dosing from clinical trials?

## Tier 2: Confidence 0.80 — Need Targeted Evidence

### 3. Wrist Flexor Stretch
Standard wrist flexor stretch with arm extended, fingers pulled back.
- Optimal hold duration and frequency from stretch research?
- Evidence for effectiveness in lateral epicondylalgia / carpal tunnel?
- Does this actually lengthen muscle tissue or primarily neurological tolerance change?

### 4. Wrist Extensor Stretch
Standard wrist extensor stretch.
- Same questions as flexor stretch
- Specific evidence for use in lateral epicondylalgia (tennis elbow)?
- Martinez-Silvestrini et al. and Bisset et al. findings on stretching vs exercise?

### 5. Forearm Pronation/Supination with Hammer
Grip hammer at end of handle, rotate forearm through full pronation/supination range.
- EMG data for pronator teres, supinator, biceps during this exercise?
- Is this used in evidence-based protocols for forearm/elbow rehab?
- Dosing recommendations from clinical trials?

### 6. Thumb Opposition Exercise
Touch thumb to each fingertip in sequence.
- EMG activation of thenar muscles during opposition tasks?
- Evidence for use in CMC OA, median nerve recovery, post-surgical hand rehab?
- Dosing and progression evidence from hand therapy literature?

### 7. Sidelying Hip Adduction
Sidelying with top leg supported, lift bottom leg.
- What % MVIC does this achieve for adductor longus, magnus, gracilis?
- Is this exercise recommended in groin pain / adductor strain rehab protocols (Copenhagen adduction alternative)?
- How does it compare to Copenhagen adductor exercise in activation levels?

### 8. Cervicothoracic Self-Mobilization
Self-mobilization techniques targeting the cervicothoracic junction.
- Evidence from Cho et al. 2017 and others on effectiveness?
- What specific techniques have RCT support?
- Dosing: frequency, duration, repetitions?
- Is this more effective than manual therapy alone?

### 9. Schroth-Based Three-Dimensional Exercises
Schroth method exercises for postural correction.
- Beyond Özdemir 2023, what other RCTs support Schroth exercises?
- Specific dosing protocols from the literature?
- Effect sizes for kyphosis reduction, pain, quality of life?
- How does it compare to general exercise for thoracic hyperkyphosis?

### 10. Hip Flexor and Hamstring Stretching
Combined hip flexor (Thomas stretch position) and hamstring stretching.
- Optimal stretch duration and frequency from systematic reviews?
- Evidence for hip flexor stretching improving lumbar lordosis or anterior pelvic tilt?
- Does hamstring flexibility actually correlate with LBP outcomes?
- Recommendations from Moreside & McGill and other biomechanics literature?

### 11. Posterior Chain Strengthening (Hamstring and Calf)
Combined hamstring curl and calf raise exercises.
- For hamstring curl: EMG comparison of prone vs seated vs Nordic
- For calf raise: single-leg vs bilateral activation differences
- Evidence-based progression from bodyweight to loaded
- Dosing for tendinopathy vs general strengthening vs post-ACL rehab

## Tier 3: Confidence 0.85 — Need Dosing/EMG Refinement

### 12. Clamshell
- Optimal resistance band placement and load for glute med activation?
- Does hip flexion angle (30° vs 45° vs 60°) change activation patterns?
- Dosing from Distefano 2009 and other EMG studies?

### 13. Calf Stretch (Wall Stretch)
- Evidence for static vs dynamic stretching for gastrocnemius/soleus?
- Optimal hold time from systematic reviews on lower limb flexibility?
- Does calf stretching actually improve ankle dorsiflexion ROM in clinical populations?

### 14. Overhead Press (Seated)
- EMG shoulder muscle activation comparison: seated vs standing?
- Is this recommended in shoulder rehab protocols, or primarily a strength exercise?
- Contraindications / modifications for impingement populations?

### 15. Resisted Internal Rotation
- EMG activation of subscapularis vs pectoralis major during banded IR?
- Dosing from rotator cuff rehab protocols (Reinold, Escamilla)?
- Is this exercise recommended for instability populations?

### 16. Tricep Extension (Overhead)
- EMG comparison of long head activation: overhead vs pushdown vs kickback?
- Dosing for rehab vs strengthening contexts?
- Shoulder stability requirements — when is this contraindicated?

### 17. Grip Strengthening (Squeeze Ball)
- EMG of FDS, FDP, intrinsics during squeeze ball vs dynamometer?
- Evidence for grip training in hand OA (Brosseau 2018 guidelines)?
- Dosing: resistance level, hold duration, frequency?

### 18. Resisted Ankle Inversion/Eversion
- EMG peroneal vs tibialis posterior during banded eversion/inversion?
- Dosing from ankle instability rehab protocols?
- Progression from isometric to isotonic to eccentric?
